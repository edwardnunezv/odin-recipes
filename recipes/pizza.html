Pizza

Description:

This is a great recipe when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella Chesse
  • Ham
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, mix 1 packet of yeast with 1 teaspoon of sugar and ¾ cup of warm water (about 110°F/43°C). Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. If it doesn’t froth, the yeast might be old or the water too hot/cold.
  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, combine 2½ cups bread flour and 1 teaspoon salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and 2 tablespoons olive oil. Stir until the dough starts to come together, then knead it on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. If it’s too sticky, add a bit more flour.
  3. Let the Dough Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Dough: Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll it out to your desired thickness, usually about ¼ inch thick. You can shape it into a round or rectangle depending on your preference.
